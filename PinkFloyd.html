David Gilmour

David Gilmour is a legendary guitarist and vocalist of Pink Floyd. He joined the band in 1968 and became a cornerstone of its iconic sound.

Roger Waters

Roger Waters is the bassist and main lyricist of Pink Floyd. He is known for his conceptual brilliance and shaping the band's identity.

Richard Wright

Richard Wright was a talented keyboardist and composer, contributing to Pink Floyd's unique soundscapes and compositions.

Syd Barrett

Syd Barrett was the founding member, guitarist, and songwriter of Pink Floyd, known for his creativity and experimental style.

Nick Mason

Nick Mason is the drummer and a founding member of Pink Floyd. His unique drumming style and contributions have been integral to the band's iconic sound throughout its history.
